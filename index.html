What I do like

Sleep everywhere

Cats sleep multiple times each day rather than in one, long period, like humans generally sleep. These cat naps average 78 minutes in length. However, cats commonly sleep for periods of time ranging from 50 to 113 minutes.

Warm up

Cats are so attracted to sunbeams because it helps them regulate their body temperature and conserve energy at the same time. The sun warms them, so they spend less metabolic energy on staying warm, and more on recharging from hunting, fighting and defending territory, and more.

Learn more

Smell shoes

Smelly shoes are likely to come with odors, including pheromones, from other cats or animals. When a cat rubs on smelly shoes, he probably wants to 'rewrite' the message on the shoes, adding his signature; he may also want to exchange signals with the owner who is a member of the same social group.
